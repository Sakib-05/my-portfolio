About Our Program

Our program connects volunteer dentists with communities in need of dental care. Sign up to make a difference and help improve dental health for those who need it most.

How It Works

Dentists can sign up to volunteer in various locations. Simply fill out the sign-up form with your details and the location where you wish to volunteer.

About Kohat, Pakistan

Nestled in the scenic Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province, Kohat is rich in tradition and natural beauty. Our initiative extends mentorship to this culturally vibrant region, uniting the global dental community in advancing oral healthcare.

Our Guiding Principles

  • Quality: We are committed to providing top-quality dental care and mentorship.
  • Accessibility: We believe in equal access to dental education and mentorship opportunities for all.
  • Faith: Trust and transparency are the cornerstones of our mentorship community.
  • Equality: We value and respect every voice in our inclusive and supportive environment.
